A cluster is a category that groups students according to their behavior within the course. For example, a cluster can be used to identify how many students have passed the first 10 lessons with a score higher than 80%. A dimension is each of the behaviors included in the cluster. The above example has 10 dimensions, one for each lesson.

Clusters can also be used to find relationships between behavior patterns and make predictions. For example, two clusters can be used to identify how many students who passed Lesson 1 with more than 80% obtained a good score on question 5 in Lesson 2.
